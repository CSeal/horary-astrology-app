## Description (max 4000 chars)

```
Ask a specific question. Get a real answer — in seconds.

Hora uses the classical horary astrology method (William Lilly, Regiomontanus houses) to cast a chart the moment you ask your question and interpret it with AI in plain language. No birth time required. No astrology knowledge needed.

HOW IT WORKS

1. Type your sincere, specific question
2. The app captures the exact moment and your location
3. A horary chart is calculated using traditional technique (Swiss Ephemeris DE431)
4. AI interprets the chart's significators and delivers a clear verdict: YES, NO, MAYBE, or UNCLEAR
5. Save the reading to your private question journal

This is horary astrology — a 600-year-old predictive technique codified by William Lilly in "Christian Astrology" (1647). Your question is answered by analyzing the chart cast for the precise moment it was asked, not by a daily sun-sign horoscope.

WHAT YOU GET

- Instant verdict: YES / NO / MAYBE / UNCLEAR with confidence level (HIGH / MEDIUM / LOW)
- AI-generated interpretation in plain language — no astrology knowledge required
- Planetary significators: the planets representing you and your question's subject, with sign, house, and dignity
- Applying aspects between significators — the key perfections that decide the verdict
- Timing hints: approximate "when" for favorable outcomes
- Chart Strength indicator (radicality score 0–100) — how reliable is this chart to judge?
- Void-of-course Moon warning when applicable, with Lilly's exception signs noted
- Question journal: all your readings saved locally on your device, grouped by month

WHY IT'S DIFFERENT

Every other horary app shows you a raw chart and expects you to interpret it yourself. Hora interprets it for you — using the same classical rulebook (essential dignities, house-based significators, reception) that a trained astrologer would use.

Other horary tools (Morinus, Janus, Solar Fire) are web-only or desktop-only. Hora is the first beautiful, native mobile horary app.

QUESTIONS PEOPLE ASK

- Will this relationship work out?
- Should I accept the job offer?
- Will I get the money I'm owed?
- Is this the right time to move?
- Will my health situation improve?
- Will the deal close?
- Should I start the new project?

Any sincere, specific yes/no question about a real situation in your life.

WHAT HORARY IS NOT

Horary astrology is not a sun-sign horoscope. It does not predict your personality or your birth chart transits. It answers one specific question by analyzing the planetary positions at the exact moment the question was asked. The technique was developed and codified by William Lilly in 17th-century England and remains the most rigorous predictive method in traditional Western astrology.

PRIVACY

All readings are stored locally on your device. No account required. No personal data collected beyond the question text, your location (used only for chart calculation), and the timestamp. Location is used only at the moment of the question and is never stored on our servers or shared with third parties beyond the chart calculation provider.

For entertainment and educational purposes only. Results are not professional advice.

LANGUAGES: English · Русский · Deutsch · Français · Español · Português · Українська
```
