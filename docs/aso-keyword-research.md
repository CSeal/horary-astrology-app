## Strategic Conclusions

1. **"horary" is a golden keyword** — volume 52 with difficulty only 25 in US. Anomalous: usually vol 50+ means difficulty 40+. The reason: no strong app has this word in their title.

2. **"astrology: horary chart"** in autocomplete (vol 40) signals another branded app. Not identified in search results — likely small/regional.

3. **Horary niche is uncontested** — Lunaton has 2 reviews in 3+ years. We enter an empty field.

4. **"vedic astrology chart"** (vol 40, diff 29) is a low-competition bridge keyword even though we're western horary, not vedic — cross-intent users searching for any serious chart app.

5. **UK locale is unique opportunity** — no competitor has Ukrainian ASO. Lunaton supports 8 languages but not Ukrainian.
