# Hora — Support

**Hora** casts a classical horary astrology chart the moment you ask a question and
interprets it with AI in plain language — an instant **YES / NO / MAYBE / UNCLEAR** verdict.
No birth time, no astrology knowledge required.

## Frequently asked questions

### How do I ask a question?
Type a sincere, specific yes/no question on the home screen and tap **Ask**. The app records the
exact moment and your location to cast the chart, then returns a verdict in seconds.

### Do I need my birth time?
No. Horary astrology answers a single question from the chart of the moment it was asked — not from
your birth chart. No birth data is needed.

### Why does the app use my location?
Your location at the moment of asking is part of the horary chart calculation. It is used only for
that calculation, is never stored on our servers, and is never shared. See the Privacy Policy.

### Where are my questions stored?
All readings are saved locally on your device, grouped by month in your journal. No account is
required and nothing is uploaded to us.

### How are the answers calculated?
Charts use the classical method (William Lilly, Regiomontanus houses, Swiss Ephemeris DE431). The
AI interprets the chart's significators, applying aspects, and essential dignities the way a
traditional astrologer would.

### Is this professional advice?
No. Hora is for entertainment and educational purposes only. Results are not professional, medical,
legal, or financial advice.
